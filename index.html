About Kupferquelle Hotel

Kupferquelle Hotel is an oasis of peace and luxury in the heart of Namibia. Our hotel offers first-class service, comfortable rooms, and an in-house restaurant with local and international specialties.

For over 50 years, we have been welcoming guests from all over the world, offering them an unforgettable experience in the breathtaking landscape of Namibia.
